Why did we create CBD Token?

Blockchain enabled crypto-currency solution for CBD sales.

CBD Token will revolutionize both the crypto-currency and medical cannabis industry by bringing a token to market that enables psuedo-anonymous transactions online and at POS locations.

The goal of CBD Token is to increase awareness and patient access to CBD without restrictions placed on the sales of CBD both offline with credit card processors and online with services such as PayPal.

Our goal is to ensure that CBD Token will become a form of payment in the medical cannabis community by early 2020.

Why did we choose the TRON Blockchain ?

Simple. No other Blockchain even comes close to the TRON Network.

Since moving to it's mainnet in summer of 2018 Tron has become a top ten crypto-currency by marketcap. With over 100,000,000,000 in supply the network is vast and full of future potential.

Imagine a world here major industries are all part of a tokenized economy. Could CBD Token make up for 1:1000th of the TRON market? We believe it's a possibility and we're planning accordingly.

02.
How is this different than using PayPal ?

PayPal explicitly restricts the sale of CBD Oil using it's platform to please it's merchant credit card processors. Because we do not require credit card processors we do not need to comply by the same regulations and therefor allow CBD product sales using CBD Token on our marketplace platform.

Customers benefit from the psuedo-anonymous nature of CBD Token which means no self identifying information is tied to the payment processor like you have when using a credit card to purchase online or via third party merchant such as PayPal. Vendors benefit from not have to worry about chargebacks.

When the time comes to list we will opt for a TRON DEX Exchange such as the Tron based exchanges that enable trading via desktop, and mobile platforms.
